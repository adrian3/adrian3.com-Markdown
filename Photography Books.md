# Photography Books
![](https://adrian3.com/images/Photography%20Books/Photography%20Books%209.jpg)

There are three books documenting my photography. The titles are:

[Motion and Distortion](https://adrian3.com/images/Photography%20Books/Photography%20Book%20Cover%20Motion%20and%20Distortion%20copy.jpg)  
[Reflection and Distraction](https://adrian3.com/images/Photography%20Books/Photography%20Book%20Cover%20Reflection%20and%20Distraction%20copy.jpg)  
[Isolation and Repetition](https://adrian3.com/images/Photography%20Books/Photography%20Book%20Cover%20Isolation%20and%20Repetition%20copy.jpg)  

Each hard cover book is 32 pages and contains 29 of what I consider to be the best photos from 15 years of experiments, accidents, and triumphs. My cameras alternate from pinhole to handmade to antique. The books are currently out of print.
