# Photography Books
![](../1.%20Art/Photography%20Books/Photography%20Books%209.jpg)

There are three books documenting my photography. The titles are:

[Motion and Distortion](../1.%20Art/Photography%20Books/Photography%20Book%20Cover%20Motion%20and%20Distortion.jpg)  
[Reflection and Distraction](../1.%20Art/Photography%20Books/Photography%20Book%20Cover%20Reflection%20and%20Distraction.jpg)  
[Isolation and Repetition](../1.%20Art/Photography%20Books/Photography%20Book%20Cover%20Isolation%20and%20Repetition.jpg)  

Each hard cover book is 32 pages and contains 29 of what I consider to be the best photos from 15 years of experiments, accidents, and triumphs. My cameras alternate from pinhole to handmade to antique. The books are currently out of print.
