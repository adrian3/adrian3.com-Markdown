# Photography Books
![](https://adrian3.com/images/Photography%20Books/Photography%20Books%209.jpg)

There are three books documenting my photography. The titles are:

[Motion and Distortion](images/Photography Books/Photography Book Cover Motion and Distortion copy.jpg)  
[Reflection and Distraction](images/Photography Books/Photography Book Cover Reflection and Distraction copy.jpg)  
[Isolation and Repetition](images/Photography Books/Photography Book Cover Isolation and Repetition copy.jpg)  

Each hard cover book is 32 pages and contains 29 of what I consider to be the best photos from 15 years of experiments, accidents, and triumphs. My cameras alternate from pinhole to handmade to antique. The books are currently out of print.
