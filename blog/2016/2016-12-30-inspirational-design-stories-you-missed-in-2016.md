# Inspirational Design Stories You Missed in 2016

---- 

There is a good chance you missed my best writing of 2016. Let's fix that. Of the 50 stories I posted this year, here are the ten that got the most traffic as well as some of the work I am proudest of. 

1. <a href="https://medium.com/@ade3/evidence-that-steve-jobs-was-aware-of-his-reality-distortion-field-49e4eacf8306">Evidence that Steve Jobs was Aware of his Reality Distortion Field</a>  
	This was my most popular post of the year thanks in part to a share by Guy Kawasaki. Find out if you have a reality distortion field, too.

2. <a href="https://medium.freecodecamp.com/a-special-hell-for-designers-like-me-5c55bd855613#.ntww7aw1i">A Special Hell for Designers Like Me</a>  
	This is the post that kicked off a 13 part series exploring the seemingly innocent compromises designers make that end up corrupting our work.

3. <a href="https://uxdesign.cc/deadly-ui-2397120f2be9">Deadly UI</a>  
	Was a design decision responsible for the tragic death of Anton Yelchin?

4. <a href="https://medium.com/swlh/got-a-brand-problem-put-a-bird-on-it-4e99ec94af37#.ewu86so3e">Got a brand problem? Put a bird on it.</a>  
	Can you solve the mystery of why there were no logos on the thousands of RVs donated to hurricane Katrina victims?

5. <a href="https://medium.com/@ade3/the-designers-invisible-skill-set-65c7bab324b6">The Designer’s Invisible Skill Set</a>  
	It often surprises me which stories catch on. This one caught the eye of The Loop and spread from there.

6. <a href="https://medium.com/@ade3/becoming-bobblehead-buddha-925eddfacc1">Becoming Bobblehead Buddha</a>  
	Why do creative people see the world differently from everyone else?

7. <a href="https://uxdesign.cc/special-hell-6-dymaxion-man-13a3cb1023aa#.nswgzss48">Special Hell 6, Dymaxion Man</a>  
	The story of my hero, Buckminster Fuller, and how a visionary crackpot  turned Earth’s rudder

8. <a href="https://medium.com/swlh/stop-dribbbling-get-a-design-education-at-costco-2021f2dd704b#.mvtbq2ws3">Stop Dribbbling, Get a Design Education at Costco</a>  
	How did I graduate from design school without learning to care?

9. <a href="https://medium.com/@ade3/the-slow-steady-inevitable-march-towards-oatmeal-e29e2ac2a10f#.62v6jgrue">The Slow, Steady, Inevitable March Towards Oatmeal</a>  
	Is your job to satisfy the demands of consumers? If that is true we are in trouble.

10. <a href="https://medium.freecodecamp.com/data-drivel-f3efc8238732?source=linkShare-f82ce6c25013-1482013363">I Am Data Drivel</a>  
	Need a New Year's resolution? Abandon Google Analytics with me.

---

Thanks to everyone who read my writing this year. If you forgot to buy me a Christmas gift, do me a favor and share your favorite story on Twitter, will you? Happy New Year. _Stay creative._
