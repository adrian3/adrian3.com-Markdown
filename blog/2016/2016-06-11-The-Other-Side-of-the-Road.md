# The Other Side of the Road

---- 

It seems overwhelming, doesn’t it? I mean that thing you are trying to do. Will it ever get done? There is so much opposition. So much red tape, complexity, and chances for it to go horribly wrong.

Getting it done would be like convincing everyone to drive on the other side of the road at the same time. Impossible.

Take 18 minutes to listen to [how Sweden transitioned from driving on the left side of the road to the right][1]. It is a remarkable story and I found it surprisingly inspiring. Massive change is possible. _Stay creative._


[1]:	http://99percentinvisible.org/episode/h-day/
